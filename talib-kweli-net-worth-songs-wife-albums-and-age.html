What is Talib Kweli Net Worth?

Talib Kweli, a rapper, entrepreneur, and activist, has a net worth of $4 million. He first gained recognition as a part of the hip-hop duo Black Star with rapper Mos Def.

Later, he had a successful solo career and worked with artists like Pharrell Williams, Kanye West, and Just Blaze.

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Talib Kweli was born in 1975 in New York City, New York, and grew up in Brooklyn’s Park Slope neighborhood.

His mother works as an English professor, and his father is an administrator at Adelphi University. He has a younger brother named Jamal.

During his youth, Kweli attended Brooklyn Technical High School and later went to Cheshire Academy in Connecticut.

He pursued higher education in experimental theater at New York University.

In 1997, Kweli made his recording debut on Mood’s album “Doom,” where he featured on several tracks.

He also collaborated with rapper and producer Hi-Tek, and together they worked on underground recordings under the name Reflection Eternal.

Some of their early tracks included “Fortified Live” and “B-Boy Document 99/Chaos.”
