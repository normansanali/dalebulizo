Bobbi Kristina Brown's Best Singing Clips Are Heartwarming &amp; Prove She Inherited Whitney Houston's T

Publish date: 2024-08-03

For Bobbi Kristina Brown, you might say that music is a family tradition. With parents like R&B singer-songwriter Bobby Brown and music icon Whitney Houston, Brown practically grew up on stage alongside her mother and other industry veterans. potentially cementing her own status as a future music legend. That being said, Brown's always made it clear that she's a talented singer and performer in her own right and isn't intimidated by her parents' legacy. During her time on The Houstons: On Our Own, a reality series documenting the Houston family following Whitney Houston's passing, the show followed Brown as she sought to pursue her own career as a singer. Though she did admit on the show that being Houston's daughter is both "a blessing and a curse," Brown seems to be know for certain that a career in music is her choice, and she's sticking to it.

(Update Feb. 4 : According to the lawyer of Bobbi Kristina Brown's father, Bobby Brown, Bobbi Kristina and Nick Gordon were not legally married.)

In light of the tragic news that broke early Saturday morning — reporting that Brown was rushed to a Georgia-area hospital after being found unresponsive at her home by her husband, Nick Gordon — fans of Brown's and Houston's are undoubtedly looking for a bit of light during this shocking time.
