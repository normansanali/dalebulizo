Adam G. Sevani is an Armenian actor and dancer who rose to fame after portraying the role of Robert Alexander III / Moose in the Step Up dance drama film series (2008, 2010, 2012, 2014). He has starred in several other movies like the drama film The Emperor’s Club (2002) and the romantic comedy The First Time (2012). Adam has appeared in various TV shows including Made in Hollywood (2010-2014) and Teen Choice Awards 2010 (2010) having been the presenter in the latter. He has also been featured in numerous music videos such as Will Smith‘s Switch (2005). Adam went on to form a dance crew with Jon Chu and a number of California area dancers with celebrity cameos in 2008 named AC/DC, or Adam/Chu Dance Crew who had the highly publicized YouTube dance fight with Miley Cyrus. Adam is the recipient of many accolades including the “Best Scene Stealer” award at the Young Hollywood Awards in 2008.
