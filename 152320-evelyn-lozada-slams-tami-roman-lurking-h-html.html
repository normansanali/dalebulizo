Evelyn Lozada Slams Tami Roman for Lurking on Her Ex-Fianc's IG Page

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Evelyn Lozada and Tami Roman have been frenemies for the longest time, and both women are back at it again as the Season 8 premiere of “Basketball Wives” approaches.

The reality stars recently had a back-and-forth on Instagram, and it all started because Evelyn did not like the fact that Tami left a comment on her former fiancé/baby daddy Carl Crawford’s Instagram post.

THE CONTROVERSIAL 'COMMENT'

According to MadameNoire, Carl, a retired baseball player-turned-record label executive, shared an Instagram post promoting his signee, Megan Thee Stallion’s debut album.

Tami liked the post and also commented with emojis of the Texas hook ’em horns hand gesture, likely in honor of her and Meg’s Houston hometown.

EVELYN LASHES OUT

But Evelyn was apparently not happy that Tami was leaving “comments” on her former lover’s face and took to her Insta Stories to vent with a cryptic post.

“You avoided me all season because you didn’t want me to EXPOSE all of your lies, but you have the nerve to be on my sons (sic) fathers page,” Evelyn wrote, as captured by The Shade Room. “THIRSTY.”

“FAMILY OVER EVERYTHING!!!!!! You tried it chicken,” she added. “We’re laughing at you!!!”

TAMI GETS DIRECT

Tami caught wind of Evelyn’s shady comments and, obviously not in the mood for games, called her out directly with a lethal clapback.

“At me next time Evelyn,” she shot back. “For clarity, I avoided you because you’re toxic.

“Secondly, I liked something that came up on my explorer page of Meg the Stallion, who I support because she’s from HTown. Thirst is you lurking on your ex’s page reading comments. He’s definitely moved on so should you. Finally I will support whoever the hell I want to Bird brain, it’s called Instagram people put [expletive] up for others to engage.”

EVELYN RESPONDS AGAIN

Evelyn was not done, however, and she fired off a lengthy response filled with accusations against Tami even though she still avoided addressing her [Tami] directly.

“I’m ‘toxic,’ but you got on national tv & fake cried to the world saying I told you I was the one that hit my ex-husband to cover your [expletive] for your domestic violence comments?!” Evelyn wrote. “You avoided me because you didn’t want me to expose all of the #FakeNews you put into the universe for attention!”

She then accused Tami of lying about various aspects of her health, including false pregnancies.

PARTING WORDS

At this point, Tami reportedly called Evelyn a psycho stalker who would have no more of her time. “I will not go back & forth with a psycho stalker who has nothing to lose!” she wrote in her Insta Stories. “Evelyn, you’re sad.”

Tami followed that with a picture from the set of the Bounce TV hit series “Saints & Sinners,” letting folks know that she’s too busy to continue with the online back-and-forth.

"Basketball Wives" will return for Season 8 in June.
