Baby Doll Coming to NWA 74 Fan Fest, More Details

Publish date: 2024-07-29

The National Wrestling Alliance is loading up the lineup for Fan Fest.

Before the pay-per-view, the promotion will offer fans the chants to meet a number of noteworthy names. The NWA has announced that Baby Doll will be doing photographs and autographs.

From NWA:

Baby Doll coming to NWA 74 Fan Fest

Chase Park Plaza Hotel, St. Louis, Missouri August 27 & 28, 2022

The National Wrestling Alliance is bringing some of the most I flier that legends to the Chase in St. Louis for NWA 74 Fan Fest.

Appearing Night 1… Baby Doll!

Best known for her work in Crockett Promotions, Baby Doll was a pillar of this territory. Baby Doll played a major role in the red hit feud between Magnum T.A. And Tully Blanchard and much more during her illustrious career. Photo and autograph opportunities will be available for purchase.

You asked, we delivered! Come meet the current stars AND legends of the National Wrestling Alliance prior to each night of NWA 74! You can purchase a one-day pass for $20 or a two-day discounted bundle for just $30! Don’t miss your chance to witness history! More legends will be announced soon!

Fan Fest Ticket Includes:

  • 30 minute Q&A with William Patrick Corgan and other NWA talent.
  • One NWA event poster to sign.
  • An exclusive piece of NWA merchandise.
    Early entry to merchandise shopping.

The NWA’s top champions, including Trevor Murdoch, Kamille, Tyrus, the Commonwealth Connection, Pretty Empowered (Ella Envy and Kenzie Paige), and Jax Dane, will also be at the Fan Fest.
