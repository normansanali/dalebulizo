AEW All-Atlantic Championship Match Set For 10/12 AEW Dynamite, Updated Card

Publish date: 2024-09-13

The October 12 episode of is set to be a big one, and it will feature a heated match for the AEW All-Atlantic Championship.

Orange Cassidy will challenge PAC for the title on Wednesday. On AEW Battle of the Belts IV, Cassidy returned, after he had been sidelined for a few weeks, and attacked PAC after the champion beat Trent Beretta to retain the gold. “The Bastard” previously defended the title against Cassidy on AEW Dynamite: Grand Slam, and “Freshly Squeezed” suffered an injury during the bout.

As confirmed during the October 7 episode of AEW Rampage, it was announced that next week’s episode of Dynamite would see Britt Baker and Jamie Hayter team up to take on the likes of AEW Interim Women’s World Champion Toni Storm and Hikaru Shida.

The match will pit Baker up against someone she’s been feuding with for some time in Storm, as Baker looks to climb back up the rankings of the and put herself back into contention for the title she previously held. Storm became the interim champion by beating Baker, Hayter, and Storm at AEW All Out. She later retained the gold in a four-way bout against Baker, Athena, and Serena Deeb on AEW Dynamite: Grand Slam.

The updated card for the October 12 episode of AEW Dynamite is as follows:

  • : Chris Jericho (c) vs. Bryan Danielson
  • : PAC (c) vs. Orange Cassidy
  • Toni Storm & Hikaru Shida vs. Britt Baker & Jamie Hayter
  • Luchasaurus (w/ Christian Cage) vs. Jungle Boy
  • Swerve Strickland vs. Billy Gunn

WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs.
