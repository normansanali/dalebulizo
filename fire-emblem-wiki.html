Fire Emblem Wiki is a wiki devoted to the Fire Emblem series, and a member wiki of the Nintendo Independent Wiki Alliance. It was created on August 26, 2010 by a user referred to as Izagar (formerly known as "Eliwood" or "Yusei"). It joined the Nintendo Independent Wiki Alliance on April 20th, 2011, the 21st anniversary of the Japanese release of Fire Emblem: Shadow Dragon & the Blade of Light, the first game in the series.
