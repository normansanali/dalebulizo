Jeff Hawes Bio, Age, Job, IG

Meet Jeff Hawes, husband of Tiffani Hawes who starred in Oprah Winfrey Network’s reality show ‘Ladies Who List: Atlanta’. The show brought viewers closer to the life of successful African-American women who juggled their careers in real estate and their personal lives. When the boss ladies earned attention with the show, their respective spouses too garnered attention.

This Jeff Hawes Bio unfolds information on Tiffani’s husband’s age, job, his IG, and other related information. So, delve into this article to learn more about him.

Meet Jeff Hawes, Ladies Who List Tiffani Hawes Husband

As stated earlier, Jeff Hawes is the husband of ‘Ladies Who List: Atlanta’ star Tiffani Hawes. Jeff and Tiffani have been married for 13 years. On 30 August 2021, his wife announced on her Instagram that had they tied the knots in 2008. Because they were financially strong they spent only $3,000 on their wedding and paid about $200 for her dress.

Tiffani announced, “At that time we had one kid which was DJ. We didn’t start with much but it is amazing how working together, staying loyal, and realizing what is important in life allows you to create something beautiful and long-lasting! It allows you to grow together, build together, amass wealth together, and create long-term wealth for your children.”

Furthermore, Tiffani ended the post, “We have done everything together! Marriage is a wonderful thing and I am thankful Jeff was checking for me at SSU. And yes on my wedding day I didn’t wear any makeup. I always like to keep it simple”.

Now, Jeff and Tiffani share three children. They have two sons; DJ Hawes and Josiah Hawes, and a daughter named JoLee Hawes. DJ, the eldest child turned 15 years old on 21 October 2021. What’s more, their son Josiah celebrated his birthday on 12 December 2021. Moreover, their daughter turned 5 years old on 11 April 2021.

The married couple is involved in community work. Furthermore, Tiffani commended him for his dedication to his life.

Jeff Hawes Age

Reportedly born in 1985, Jeff Hawes turned 36 years old in 2021. He celebrates his birthday on 7 July.

Jeff Hawes Job

According to the Facebook post of Tiffani from July 2021, Jeff Hawes earned his license to become a builder. She announced, “Major shout out to my husband who is no longer just a hashtag #builderjeff. He is now a fully LICENSED BUILDER!!! Set your mind to it and do it! He passed on the first try! #licensedbuilderjeff”.

Indeed reports that the average salary of a builder is $28.66 in Georgia.

Bizapedia reported that Jeff is the owner of J Hawes Real Estate LLC in Georgia. He and his wife registered the company on 7 August 2018. Moreover, he also has a LinkedIn profile which states that he worked for Gwinnett Place Honda as a service manager.

Tiffani, his wife, was a graduate of Savannah State University with a BS in political science who was planning to attend law school in Texas. When she found out she was pregnant with her first kid, she diverged from her initial plan. Tiffani subsequently postponed law school for a while and attended Augusta State University to pursue a paralegal certificate. She completed her paralegal certificate in a year and Bush Law Business, PC, a real estate closing law firm recruited her, where she earned $9.00 per hour.

Then, from 2007 to 2008, Tiffani worked there for a year. She went on to work as a Real Estate Paralegal at Fletcher, Harley & Fletcher, LLP, earning $30,000 per year. She worked as a Real Estate Closing Coordinator/Paralegal for Wilson and Associates, LLC from March 2009 to December 2012.

Also, Jeff lost his job during the 2008 financial crisis, and it was the ideal time for them to relocate to Atlanta so she could pursue her law school goals. Jeff landed a new job after they moved to Atlanta, and John Marshall Law School accepted Tiffani, where she began night school in the fall of 2010.

She earned her JD in Sports and Entertainment in 2013.

Moreover, Tiffani worked as a legal intern for three months at The Riley Law Firm, LLC while in college. She passed the bar test in February 2014 after graduation, and she launched The Hawes Law Firm in June 2014. Since then, Tiffani has served as the company’s president and a real estate closing attorney.
