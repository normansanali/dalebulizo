Vanessa Bryants Photos With Trae Young Go Viral

Publish date: 2024-07-26

Vanessa Bryant’s photos with Atlanta Hawks star Trae Young went viral this week.

Vanessa, the matriarch of the Bryant family, has served as a symbol of strength and grace in the face of tragedy ever since Kobe Bryant’s untimely passing.

Not only has Vanessa done everything imaginable to keep Kobe and Gianna’s memories alive, but she has done so while facing trials and tribulations that few could understand — like a legal battle with her own mother.

This week, Vanessa found herself in the headlines once again.

On Sunday, Vanessa shared photos of her exclusive court reopening at Wilson Park in honor of Kobe and Gianna. Young was one of the main dignitaries in attendance, as was WNBA legend Lisa Leslie.

Vanessa is obviously no stranger to making headlines. Between her intimate photos causing a stir, her outfit for NBA All-Star Weekend setting the internet ablaze and her most recent photos with Pau Gasol, she has been a mainstay in the news cycle.

This week was no exception.

Young is no different. He currently has four teams vying for his services if the Atlanta Hawks agree to trade him, with one clear frontrunner slowly emerging from the pack.
