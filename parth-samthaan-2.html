Career

Parth Samthaan has become one of the famous Indian actors. He gained recognition in the Indian film fraternity after playing the role of Manik Malhotra in Kaisi Yeh Yaariyan. While that happened to be his first project, Parth has been featured in many episodes of Savdhaan India, Yeh Hai Aashiqui, and Pyar Tune Kya Kiya. He has mainly hosted these shows. He got his first break in “Best friends Forever” where he was starred opposite Charlie Chauhan. The show was aired on Channel V.

He got the best career opportunity in Kaisi Yeh Yaariyan. Parth worked alongside Charlie Chauhan and Niti Taylor for the show. It became one of the successful shows of the year. Parth and Niti reunited for another season of Kaisi Yeh Yaariyan. Parth got a chance to work in the Bollywood industry with the movie “Googly Ho Gayi”. It was his debut movie that was released in 2016. Parth Samthaan sang “Jind Meri”.

Two years later, he was featured in the Kaisi Yeh Yaariyan’s last sequel. Parth got another major project in 2019 in the daily soap “Kasauti Zindagi Kay”. Parth worked with Erica Fernandez. Samthaan got nominated for the Best Jodi popular award at the Indian Telly Award. Parth worked alongside his Kasauti Zindagi Kay co-actor “Pooja Banerjee” in Alt Balaji’s show Kehne Ko Humsafar Hain. Samthaan is expected to be starred in “Mai Hero Bol Raha Hun”.
