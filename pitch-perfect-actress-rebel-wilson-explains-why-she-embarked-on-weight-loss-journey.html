'Pitch Perfect' Actress Rebel Wilson Explains Why She Embarked On Weight-Loss Journey

Publish date: 2024-10-02

This past Friday, Pitch Perfect actress Rebel Wilson took to Instagram to give an update about her “Year of Health” journey to fans. Wilson shared two photos of herself wearing a beautiful navy blue dress, thanking fans for their support.

Fans showed their love to the actress, and her fellow Pitch Perfect cast members praised her weight-loss success - seemingly at a loss for words at her progress. Anna Camp, who played Aubrey in the film, commented pink hearts with the blue arrow pierced in the middle. Brittany Snow, who played Chloe, commented, "Rebs!" with several fire emojis.

Earlier this year, the actress opened up about her 2020 weight-loss goals. Wilson is best known for playing roles that center around her body size; She revealed to The Sun that her change in lifestyle steams from being paid to “stay bigger” by film directors.

“It's not like I want to lose weight and get to around a certain number. It's more than that, it's about dealing mentally with why I was overeating and I had a job where I was paid a lot of money to be bigger, at times which kind of can mess with your head a bit,” she said.

To achieve this helath goal, Wilson has been working with personal trainer Jono Castano. For her diet, Wilson has been following the Mayr Method diet plan. After visiting VivaMayr, a luxury medical detox and wellness center in Austria, she was fascinated by the plan.

The diet plan was created by Dr. Franz Xaver Mayr. The nearly 100-year-old diet is centered around whole foods with high alkaline content, like vegetables, sheep’s milk yogurt, and fresh fish.

In addition to sharing her weight loss journey, Wilson took the time to highlight her favorite charitable cause. She is helping raise money for The School of St. Jude, a charity-funded school that provides free quality education to 1800 low-income students in Arusha, Tanzania.

Wilson's weight loss is an inspiration to many, especially those who want to lead a healthier lifestyle without feeling like weight loss is at the center of it. Fans and friends alike can look forward to seeing where her journey takes her in the future.
