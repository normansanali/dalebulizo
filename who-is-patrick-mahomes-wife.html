Who is Patrick Mahomes Wife?

Publish date: 2024-07-12

Patrick Mahomes, the star quarterback of the Kansas City Chiefs, is married to Brittany Matthews, an athlete and entrepreneur. Their relationship has garnered attention for various reasons, including Brittany's friendship with pop star Taylor Swift. Here's a detailed overview of Patrick Mahomes' wife, Brittany Matthews, covering her background, career, relationship with Patrick, and her friendship with Taylor Swift.

Background and Career

Brittany Matthews, born on August 31, 1995, in Whitehouse, Texas, is an accomplished athlete in her own right. She was a soccer star in college and later founded her own company, Brittany Lynne Fitness, which offers guided workout programs.
In 2021, she co-founded the Kansas City Current, an expansion team in the National Women’s Soccer League, demonstrating her entrepreneurial skills and dedication to sports.

Relationship with Patrick Mahomes

Brittany and Patrick Mahomes have been together since 2012, having met and become friends in high school. Their relationship blossomed, and they got engaged in 2020, with Patrick proposing to her in a VIP box at Arrowhead Stadium in Kansas City, Missouri.
The couple tied the knot on March 12, 2022, in Hawaii in a ceremony surrounded by family and close friends.
They welcomed their first child, a daughter named Sterling Skye, in February 2021, and their son, Patrick Lavon Mahomes III (Bronze), was born in November 2022.

Friendship with Taylor Swift

Brittany's friendship with pop star Taylor Swift has attracted significant attention. The two women have been seen together at Kansas City Chiefs games and have been described as "working on building their relationship" outside of NFL game days.
Their friendship has been highlighted in the media, with reports indicating that they have grown even closer over the past several months and share a genuine bond, often hanging out at games and cheering on their respective partners.

Brittany Matthews is not only known as the wife of NFL star Patrick Mahomes but also as an accomplished athlete, entrepreneur, and influencer. Her relationship with Patrick, their growing family, and her friendship with Taylor Swift have made her a notable figure beyond her association with the NFL.
