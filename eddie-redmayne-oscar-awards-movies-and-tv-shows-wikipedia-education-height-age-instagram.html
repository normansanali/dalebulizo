Eddie Redmayne Oscar Awards

How many Oscar Awards have been won by Eddie Redmayne? Eddie Redmayne has been nominated for two Oscars (Academy Awards) but he has won only one of them.

Below are his Oscar nominations and award:

  • Best Actor Award (The Theory of Everything) – WINNER 
  • Best Actor Award (The Danish Girl) – NOMINEE 

Eddie Redmayne has won other prominent awards including;

  • Best Actor in a Leading Role (The Theory of Everything) – BAFTA Awards (2015)
  • Best Actor (The Theory of Everything) – Golden Globe Award (2015)
