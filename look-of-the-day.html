Priyanka Chopra Just Proved Winter Layers Can Be Elegant and Refined

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Priyanka Chopra never fails when it comes to fashion. Just look at the bold getup she recently wore to a racing event or her “Burnin’ Up”-inspired red dress for proof. Every look simply makes me swoon. But she doesn’t only do elevated attire well; she does casual wear just as good.

Yesterday morning, Chopra wore chic winter layers. Her ensemble included a long black coat — which everyone needs at least one of in their wardrobes — alongside black sheer tights, sneaker-like booties, a star-adorned beanie, cool-girl aviators, and a sleek white purse. But the real star of the show was undoubtedly the actress’ two-piece set.

Prettygarden Oversized Sweater and Skirt Set

Anrabess Cropped Sweater and Skirt Set

The knitted outfit included a oversized teal sweater layered over a white shirt and paired with a coordinating calf-grazing maxi skirt. Each separate featured a cozy ribbed material, which really made this look pop. Not to mention, the textured fabric is extra cozy, which I can only imagine is equal parts comfy.

Chopra’s look isn’t only perfect for cool, dropping temperatures, but it’s a feminine take on winter clothing. This time of year, bulky coats, huge pants, and clunky boots take over the fashion scene, but Chopra is proving you can master chilly weather in a more refined way. Take it even further by opting for a fitted set, like this Anrabess cropped sweater option, and style it with polished stilettos. Or, stick to an oversized style, such as this Prettygarden $41 two-piece find, and spruce it up with a pair of heeled, knee-high boots.   

Similar coordinating ribbed sets can be worn just about anywhere. Take one for a stroll around town like Chopra, dress it down for a lowkey coffee date, or add some exciting accessories for a date night. It’s versatile and fool-proof, giving you a one-and-done kind of style that takes little to no effort. On the other hand, separate the pieces to create standalone outfits perfect for mixing and matching with items you already own.
