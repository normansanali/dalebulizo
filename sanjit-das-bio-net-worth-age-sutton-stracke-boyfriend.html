Meet Sanjit Das, Sutton Stracke Boyfriend! His Bio, Net Worth, Age

Publish date: 2024-09-18

Jeopardy! fans might remember Sanjit Das from the time when he bagged a total of $1,800 in April 2001, but Real Housewives of Beverly Hill fans only got to know him as Sutton Stracke’s boyfriend in 2022.

So, if you’re one of them, new to this humble man, this Sanjit Das Bio is just for you. 

Meet Sanjit Das, RHOBH Star Sutton Stracke Boyfriend

Sanjit Das was the talk amongst RHOBH fans after Sutton Stracke took it to her IG to share a lovely picture of them captioned, “Good things do come out of #rhobh” in August 2022. Amongst the comments, Crystal Kung Minkoff (Sutton’s co-star) also boasted, “I take ALL CREDIT,” and the section was flooded with blessings for the pair.

In case you missed the RHOBH episode where Sanjit was first introduced, Crystal was one of the several cast members who bore witness to an awkward FaceTime call between Sanji and Sutton during a group get-together. Long story short, “Excuse me, I actually like him,” Sutton blurted out as her phone was passed among Crystal and Erika Jayne.

But prior to this ep, Sutton had already revealed that she first met Sanjit through a dating app. The couple then eventually went on a dinner date which was filmed for the Bravo reality series. There, they spoke about movies and books and bonded over a love for college football.

“I like Sanjit a lot and we’re friends. I went out with him a couple of weeks ago and I was like, ‘Sanjit, you’re kind of cute.’ It’s confusing. He might be a slow burn,” the star shared after spending time with his new boo.

As for their second date, Sanjit and Sutton met at a posh pizza restaurant, shared an awkward kiss, and ordered drinks and food.

Prior to Sanjit, Sutton dated her boyfriend Michael for quite a while until they called it quits on Valentine’s Day weekend in 2021. To be precise, it was Michael who broke up with her saying,” I just can’t.”

Also, Sutton suffered one more heartbreak before Michael. She divorced her husband Christian Stracke in 2016 after nearly 17 years of marriage.

“It’s prickly. It’s not fun and it’s not light. All I want is a boyfriend,” Sutton once lamented.

Well, she finally has one! And it’s not just us who are hopeful about the two. Multiple RHOBH fans praised their relationship whilst also noting Sanjit’s vibe of not being “camera hungry.” They also tagged him to be “sweet, level-headed, and balanced.”

Sanjit Das Age

Sanjit Das was 56 years of age when he appeared on Real Housewives of Beverly Hill in 2022. He is six years older than Sutton (born: September 20, 1971).

This charming man celebrates his birthday in March and is either of Pisces or Aries zodiac.

Sanjit Das Job

Sanjit Das is the founder and executive producer of Moloko Entertainment. He is a  storyteller at heart who’s received multiple Emmy awards for storytelling.

Prior to this, Sanjit oversaw all aspects of content development and production (creative, distribution, branded, and social) for Harris Blitzer Sports and Entertainment (2017 – 2019) with a portfolio including the Philadelphia 76ers, New Jersey Devils, and the Prudential Center.

Also, from 2015 to 2017, Sanjit worked as the Director of Development (writing and producing VR content) at Clever Fox Media.

This talented storyteller even ran the digital content team at ABC (from 2009 to 2013), owning multi-platform experiences for over 40 primetime shows including LOST, Desperate Housewives, Grey’s Anatomy, Scandal, and The Oscars.

Furthermore, he produced digital features for one of the earliest user-generated video competition shows at NBC. com, and took the job of managing the writing and production team for MSN in 2006.

For less than a year, in 2006, Sanjit also worked as a digital showrunner at Microsoft.

As to kickstart his career, he worked as a feature development assistant at DreamWorks Pictures and executive assistant to CEO at Imagine Entertainment & Television in 1999.

In 2002, he then moved to be an assistant to the head of the Creative Artists Agency.
