Beautiful Girls is a popular song by Jamaican-American singer Sean Kingston, released in 2007.

The song was a huge commercial success, topping the charts in several countries, including the United States, United Kingdom, and Australia.

Legacy and Influence“Beautiful Girls” is often cited as one of the most iconic songs of the late 2000s. It has been covered by several artists and has been featured in several movies and TV shows. It also helped launch Sean Kingston’s career as a recording artist
