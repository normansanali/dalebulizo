Eric Bischoff Drags Darby Allin vs Sammy Guevaras Bad Finish On AEW Dynamite

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Darby Allin is also one of the top stars in AEW and has cemented himself as one of the Four Pillars in the company. His tag team with Sting remains one of the highlights of AEW television as well. It seems Eric Bischoff isn’t a fan of a recent AEW match featuring Darby Allin.

Darby Allin is a former TNT Champion as he beat Cody Rhodes to win the title last year. He had numerous matches as he defended the championship on a regular basis before dropping it to Miro.

After a lot of buildup over the past couple of weeks, Darby Allin challenged Sammy Guevara for the TNT Championship in the main event of this week’s episode of AEW Dynamite. The two had an intense back and forth match as fans were on the edge of their seats throughout.

However, Andrade El Idolo and Matt Hardy interfered in the match, costing Darby Allin the match as Guevara successfully retained the title. Eric Bischoff took to Twitter and expressed his disappointment at the ending of the match, as he was already a fan of the match prior to the interference.

Really enjoying this @sammyguevara @DarbyAllin match.

The finish sucked. Too bad.

We will have to wait and see what is next in store for Sammy Guevara, as it is clear Darby Allin will now be feuding with Andrade El Idolo and Matt Hardy in the coming weeks.
