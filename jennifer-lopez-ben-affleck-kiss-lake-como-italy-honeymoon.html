Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck Kiss During Lake Como Honeymoon

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Mr. and Mrs. Affleck's Italian honeymoon is straight out of a rom-com.

This week, the newlyweds were seen sharing a passionate kiss while on a stroll around Lake Como, where they went on holiday following their lavish wedding last weekend.

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck married in a glamorous all-white ceremony at Affleck's estate in Riceboro, Georgia, on Saturday, and celebrated the affair by jetting off on a getaway to Europe—this time, it appears, without their kids.

Lopez looked radiant in a deep yellow summer dress with billowing sleeves and an open back from FARM Rio. And to make sure she was comfortable walking around the charming town with her new husband, she switched her usual wedges for a pair of golden thong sandals and accessorized with golden aviator sunglasses.

Affleck also wore an elevated summer outfit, made up of a beige button-down, which he rolled at the sleeves; black trousers; matching lace-up boots; and dark aviator sunglasses.

The two were photographed taking in the stunning lake views along the boardwalk and embracing in front of the water. Lopez wrapped her arms around Affleck—who leaned against the railing around the lake—and stood on her tiptoes to kiss him.

The duo stayed at friend and fellow actor George Clooney's home in the area during their short visit, and on Thursday, they moved on to Milan.

They were seen looking as in love as ever during a shopping trip in the busy city yesterday. In photos taken outside the Brunello Cucinelli store, both Affleck and Lopez are smiling and greeting fans who spotted them walking into the shop.

For the outing, Lopez continued showing off her bridal looks, opting for a casual all-white ensemble. Affleck was cool in classic blue jeans and a matching button-down shirt over a white tee.
