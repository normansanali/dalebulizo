How many states is BKFC legal in?

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Bare Knuckle Fighting Championship (BKFC) has rapidly gained popularity and legal acceptance in the United States. Initially, professional bare-knuckle boxing was not legal under any federal or state laws in the country. However, significant changes have occurred, leading to the legalization of these events in multiple states. As of now, BKFC is legal and sanctioned in 25 states across the US, marking a significant milestone in the acceptance of this combat sport.
