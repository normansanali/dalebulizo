Biography

Shelley Long was born in Fort Wayne, IN on August 23, 1949. Actress best known for her Emmy and Golden Globe-winning role as the bookish barmaid Diane Chambers on the hit series Cheers. Her film credits include Night Shift, Outrageous Fortune, TThe Brady Bunch Movie and Irreconcilable Differences, the latter of which earned her a Golden Globe nomination for Best Actress. She co-starred with Ted Danson on the NBC series Cheers. She studied drama at Northwestern University and began her career as an actress in commercials. She then joined The Second City comedy troupe in Chicago.
