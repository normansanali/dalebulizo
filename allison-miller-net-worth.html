Allison Miller net worth:

$5 million

Allison Miller is an American actress. Her acting career started in the early 2000s. During her initial years she landed one-episode roles on various TV shows. This includes General Hospital, CSI: NY, Desperate Housewives, and Boston Legal. Her first main role on the small screen was on Kings, which only aired on TV for one season. She also ventured in films, appearing in the movies Blood: The Last Vampire and 17 Again. In the 2010s, she had main roles on the short-lived shows Terra Nova and Go On. She went on to star in the films Devil’s Due and There’s Always Woodstock. After a recurring role on the series Selfie, she landed a main role on the show Incorporated.
