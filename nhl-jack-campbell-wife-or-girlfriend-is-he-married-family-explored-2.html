Jack Campbell is an American ice hockey player playing for the Toronto Maple Leafs of the National Hockey League (NHL).

The Dallas Stars picked him in the first round of the 2010 NHL Entry Draft. As an international, he has represented the United States at junior as well as senior levels.

Whatsmore, he was a member of the 2010 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships winning US team. He also won the IIHF U18 World Championships twice, 2009 and 2010. He also won bronze as a senior at the 2015 Men’s Ice Hockey World Championships.
