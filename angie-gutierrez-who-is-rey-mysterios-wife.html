Angie Gutierrez: Who Is Rey Mysterios Wife?

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Angie Gutierrez is well-known as the wife of Rey Mysterio.

Angie is an American actress.

She was born on May 2, 1970 in Houston, Texas, United States.

As an actress, Angie is known for Lucha Underground (2014), WWE Raw (1993) and Summerslam (2005).

Angie Gutierrez and Rey Mysterio married in
1996.

Her husband Rey Mysterio is an American professional
wrestler who is currently signed to WWE, where he performs on the SmackDown brand.

Mysterio commenced his wrestling career at age 14 in Mexico where was trained by his uncle Rey Misterio Sr.

He joined World Championship Wrestling and Consejo Mundial de Lucha Libre before heading on to World Wrestling Entertainment in 2002.

Mysterio has held 24 championships between WWE and WCW (including three world championships). In addition, he is WWE’s 21st Triple Crown and Grand Slam champion.

Through their union, Angie and Rey share two children; Dominik Mysterio and Aalyah Gutierrez.

Their son Dominik was born on April 5, 1997, while Aalyah their daughter was born on August 20, 2001.
