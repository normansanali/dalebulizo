BiPs Astrid Loch, Kevin Wendts Relationship Timeline

Publish date: 2024-09-14

Astrid Loch and Kevin Wendt, found their happily ever after following Bachelor in Paradise season 5.

While the twosome briefly split before the season wrapped, they got back together shortly after leaving the beach.

“It was the craziest scenario that you can meet someone in, and then the breakup was the best thing that ever happened to us because it just let us kind of end the TV show chapter there in Mexico and come out of it and just work on it on our own and kind of figure out what we wanted to do,” Loch told E! News in August 2019 while reflecting on their journey.

Wendt noted at the time that the duo “just enjoy each other’s company.” He explained, “I feel like we’re on our own little island from the Bachelor world over here [in Canada], which is actually kind of nice. We get left alone a lot, but we also spend most of our time inside, to be honest.”
