Man Killed In Morning Temple Hills Shooting

Publish date: 2024-09-17

The unidentified male victim was pronounced dead on the scene around 11:30 a.m., on the 4400 block of 23rd Parkway, Thursday, June 2, Prince George's Police say.

Detectives are on scene working to establish suspect(s) and motive in this case. If anyone has any information, please call 1-866-411-TIPS.

This is a developing story and will be updated as new information is released.
