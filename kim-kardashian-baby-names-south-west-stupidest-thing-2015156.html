Kim Kardashian Talks Baby Names: South West Is &quot;the Stupidest Thing&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-11

It looks like Southwest Airlines is safe, because Kim Kardashian has no plans to name her future child after another direction. The pregnant-again reality star, 34, opened up about potential names for her second baby during an appearance on NPR’s “Wait Wait… Don’t Tell Me!” broadcast on Saturday, June 13. 

When the host began suggesting direction-centric names in foreign languages to keep true to the Kardashian’s “K-name” rule, the Selfish author quickly stopped him. 

“I don’t like South West, though, because North will always be better and she has a better direction,” Kanye West’s wife said. “So I wouldn’t do that. I don’t think we’ll go with another direction. Everyone keeps on saying South, and I think it’s so stupid. It’s the stupidest thing ever.”
