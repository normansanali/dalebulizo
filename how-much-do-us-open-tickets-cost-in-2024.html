How much do US Open tickets cost in 2024?

Publish date: 2024-07-29

The 2024 US Open, set to be held at the Pinehurst Resort & C.C. (Course No. 2) in the Village of Pinehurst, N.C. from June 10-16, is an eagerly anticipated event for golf enthusiasts. With a range of ticket options available, fans have the opportunity to choose from various experiences, each offering unique benefits and access to different facilities.

Ticket Categories and Pricing Tiers

Daily Ticket Options

  • Daily Trophy Club

    • Offers premium access to a sports bar and lounge atmosphere with various seating options and food and beverage choices for purchase.
    • Provides live television coverage throughout the facility and access to executive restrooms.
    • Includes all access that a Gallery Ticket receives.
    • Price: Available for purchase.

  • Daily Garden

    • Provides flexibility of private space and restroom access, offering a premium open-air sports bar style facility with live television coverage throughout the facility.
    • Includes executive restrooms and all access that a Gallery Ticket receives.
    • Price: Available for purchase.

  • Daily Reserved Grandstand

    • Grants access to a reserved grandstand space at a to-be-determined location at Pinehurst No. 2.
    • Provides admissions to the grounds and access to a grandstand with premium views of the course.
    • Includes all access that a Gallery Ticket receives.
    • Price: Available for purchase.

  • Daily Gallery

    • Offers grounds access for fans to see all the action and follow the world’s best players at the 124th U.S. Open.
    • Provides the ability to walk the course and sit in various grandstands, with food and beverage available at concession stands.
    • Price: Available for purchase.

    • Premium Ticket Packages

  • 1895 Club - Daily & Packages

    • The most exclusive premium ticket available for the 2024 U.S. Open, offering a luxury, climate-controlled hospitality area with an outdoor patio and dedicated tiered seating exclusive to 1895 Club guests.
    • Includes on-site Valet parking pass, all-day gourmet dining options, top-shelf open bar, and access to executive restrooms exclusive to 1895 Club guests.
    • Price: Available for purchase.

  • The Garden Weekly Ticket Package

    • A weeklong ticket package providing one (1) Garden Ticket each day Monday – Sunday of the Championship.
    • Includes a $25 loaded food & beverage bonus per day per package, along with executive restrooms and all access that a Gallery Ticket receives.
    • Price: Available for purchase.

  • Gallery Weekly Ticket Package

    • A weeklong ticket package providing one (1) Gallery Ticket each day Monday – Sunday of the Championship.
    • Includes general admission to the grounds of Pinehurst No. 2, the ability to walk the course and sit in various grandstands, along with food and beverage available at concession stands.
    • Price: Available for purchase.

    • Sold Out Categories and Corporate Hospitality

  • Trophy Club Weekly Ticket Package

    • This category is already sold out, providing access to a premium climate-controlled space along the 14th hole with various seating options and premium food and beverage choices available for purchase.

  • Corporate Hospitality

    • The corporate hospitality program offers several package levels designed to meet companies' needs and budgets, both large and small.
    • All-inclusive hospitality options are available on a weekly and daily basis, with limited inventory remaining.

    • Additional Factors Influencing Ticket Prices

    • Limited Time Offer: A limited-time offer provides the opportunity to purchase four (4) Daily Trophy Club tickets to a specific day and receive one (1) Daily VIP Parking Pass for that specific day, with limited quantities available.

    • VIP Parking Pass: The purchase of four (4) Daily Trophy Club tickets to a specific day also includes one (1) Daily VIP Parking Pass for that specific day. This offer is subject to limited quantities and a one VIP parking pass per order.

    The 2024 US Open offers a variety of ticket options, catering to different preferences and providing access to unique experiences. From premium daily ticket options to exclusive weekly ticket packages, fans have the opportunity to immerse themselves in the excitement of one of the most prestigious golf events. Additionally, limited-time offers and sold-out categories add an element of exclusivity to the ticket purchasing experience, making the 2024 US Open an event to look forward to for both golf enthusiasts and corporate entities.

    For further details and to secure tickets for the 2024 US Open, interested individuals and corporate entities can visit the official website for ticket purchases and corporate hospitality options.
